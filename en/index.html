Who we are

Bangkok Brass Band is an amateur group of wind brass based in Bangkok, Thailand. We formed this brass band in August 2011 with 9 members, and now we have grown to around 50 members.
Most of members are Japanese who are living in Thailand, but non-Japanese is also welcomed! Let's enjoy music together!

Main activities

Concert (twice a year)
Invited performance in Bangkok or city near Bangkok
